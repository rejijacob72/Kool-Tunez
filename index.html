Categories – By Instrument
  • Vocal
  • String Instruments

Grouping / Eligibility
  • Group 1: Grades 1 to 7
  • Group 2: Grades 8 to 12
  • Enrolled based on current grade
  • Group Band: Ages 1–12
  • Minimum 2, maximum 5 members
Music Selection
  • Indian Classical
  • Indian Folk
  • Indian Regional Film Songs
  • Indian Devotional
  • Indian-Western Fusion
  • Must retain clear Indian melodic or rhythmic influence
  • One piece or two short contrasting pieces
Performance Time Limit
  • Allowed time: 3–5 minutes
  • Must stay within the allotted time
Accompaniment
  • ALLOWED
  • Recorded backing tracks (without melody line)
  • One live accompanist (optional)
  • NOT ALLOWED
  • Pre-recorded lead melody
  • Karaoke
  • Accompaniment that dominates the performance
Guidelines
  • Participants must bring their own instrument
  • Limited setup time on stage
  • Memorization encouraged, not mandatory
  • Music stands must be brought by participant
  • Brief piece intro (name/raga/composer) optional but encouraged

Our Story

The Sound of
St. Louis

Kool Tunez is St. Louis's most distinctive orchestra — a collective of world-class musicians who believe orchestral music should move you, surprise you, and stay with you long after the last note fades.

Founded with a singular vision: to fuse the discipline of classical orchestration with the warmth of soul, jazz, and R&B. We perform original compositions alongside reimagined classics, creating an experience that resonates across generations and genres.

From intimate chamber evenings to grand full-orchestra spectacles, every Kool Tunez performance is crafted to be unforgettable.
